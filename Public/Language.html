Language and me

Although this portfolio is chiefly meant to demonstrate my competence within the area of web development, I thought of taking a moment to mention this nonetheless. As an avid reader, I already had hints that language was gonna become one of my greatest passions, but I only discovered its true extent once I moved to the Netherlands.

Coming from the South of Italy, I had very limited exposure to other cultures, save through books, and then the internet. I didn't know then that being able to communicate with people from other countries and speaking a different languages was going to be like gazing into the infinite.

What I found in the Netherlands surpassed even my wildest expectations. Being suddenly in contact with people from so many different realities made me want more and more. In time I realized I had a particular fascination with the Germanic languages. I'm still deeply interested in them, and right now I'm busy perfecting Dutch and English, while slowly making my way through Swedish. And I have no intention to stop anytime soon!
