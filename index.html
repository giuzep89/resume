Summary

During my youth years, I lived and breathed technology. My passion for videogames extended to computers and what made them tick, so that I quickly became the "tech guy" among my circle of friends. Later on I decided to pursue my interest in music and languages by moving to the Netherlands, where I've been able to go through a wealth of enriching experiences. As I noticed that language and technology find a natural meeting point in coding, my fascination for development sparked. I'm incredibly enthusiastic to now have the chance to explore this new path and seize the opportunities that lie therein.
